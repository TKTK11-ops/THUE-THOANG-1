 Modern & family friendly 3 DOUBLE bedroom apartment ,one bedroom with ensuite shower room additional two bedrooms one with king size bed and TV and main bathroom next to it, modern apartment within period terrace just 15mins TRAIN RIDE from Oxford Circus/Bond Street tube and 5 mins from Nottinghill Gate, The apartment is located on a tree lined street about 8mins walk from White City tube station (CENTRAL LINE) the apartment itself is a maisonette apartment (on two floors) with the kitchen open plan & reception, bathroom and bedroom on the first floor and a further two bedrooms and ensuite shower room on the second floor. Benefitting from a spacious kitchen/diner/reception with wooden floors consisting of a large widescreen LCD TV with 80 channels Dishwasher, Washing Machine, Fridge Freezer, Coffee maker, iron & ironing board, kettle, toaster, power shower and FIBRE OPTIC FAST Broadband /WIFI by Virgin Media. The apartment will comfortably accommodate 6 persons in three double bedrooms.


 Located on the first and second floor of this Edwardian terrace the house is about a 8min walk to White City tube station opposite the famous BBC television studios and a further 2mins away from Westfield shopping centre homes to hundreds of shops and bars/cafes & Wood Lane tube station. The location is ideal for the West End with Bond Street being 5 stops by tube taking approx. 16mins, Notting hill is 2 stops in 5mins. The location is also very easily accessible to Kensington Gardens & Palace and the open spaces of Hyde park. It is also on the right side of the city if you are looking to escape to the Cotswolds , Wales & the South West and offers easy access to the Harry Potter studios and Heathrow Airport. Parking is available at an hourly cost of 2.20 from 9am -5pm mon-sat. Spaces outside the flat are plenty.(there is a standard flight of stairs to access the first floor apartment which does not have a handrail fitted and another flight of stairs to the second floor bedrooms with a rail assist we also have baby gates if required and baby cot & highchair). Our apartment is light and airy with blackout blinds/New England style shutters on all bedrooms. .
